Overview

Purpose

This website is for a whitewater rafting company. The purpose of this site is to provide information about the company and the services they offer. The site will also allow users to book trips and contact the company.

Audience

The audience for this site is people who are interested in whitewater rafting. Adventerous people who are looking for a fun and exciting experience.
