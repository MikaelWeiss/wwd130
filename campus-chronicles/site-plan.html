More Info About Campus Chronicles

What is Campus Chronicles?

Campus Chronicles is a website dedicated to connecting friends and helping people remember the good times in life. This website would be a fun little place for a friend group to post articles and columns each week, keeping everyone laughing and up-to-date. Memory keeping is always hard, but this site will be so fun and easy to use it'd be harder not to use it.

How to use Campus Chronicles

Each user of Campus Chronicles will have their own account. This will allow them to make groups with their friends and post articles and columns. You can create your own free account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. Once you have an account, you can create a group and invite your friends to join. Once you have a group, you can post articles and columns for your friends to see. Every participant of a group will also be able to post articles.

The Home Page

Campus Chronicles is hand-tailored to each user. You can customize your profile with a profile picture and a bio. You can also customize your group with a group picture and a group bio. These will appear on the main page under "Team" once added.

Build An Article

Building an article couldn't be simpler. Just add in a catchy title, an optional image, and some text! Once ready, click “submit” and your post will be added to the site for all your friends to see!

Archive

Welcome to the Archive! This is the place where articles over three months old go for safe keeping. This is an especially fun place to look through old memories

Your Profile

Welcome to your profile! This is where you add in the details that will show on the home page. Below you can add details including a photo, your name, Minecraft gamertag, and a short bio. Remember to write the bio in third person! It makes it more fun

More about Campus Chronicles

Activities that visitors will want to complete on this site will include adding new articles, viewing older articles, as well as viewing information about each member of the group.

Necessary Information

Users will need to know how to navigate the site, add arctics, and update their profile. They will also need to be able to see how to make groups and invite people to those groups. If a user is a part of multiple groups, then they'll be able to see all the groups they're a part of.

Why they'll come to Campus Chronicles

You see, Campus Chronicles will be so fun to use. There are other websites where individuals can blog, or where multiple people can add to a blog, but none of them are formatted as if the editors were editors of a news paper. This added aspect makes the site so much more fun to use than other systems out there.
